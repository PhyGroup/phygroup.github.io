Biography

Dr. Huang is an assistant professor of School of Cyber Science and Engineering at Zhengzhou University (ZZU). He received his Ph.D. degree in School of Electronic Information and Communications from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2021, supervised by Prof. Wei Wang. His research is concerned with network security and wireless sensing. His papers have appeared in several premier journals and top conferences, such as IEEE/ACM TON and IEEE INFOCOM.

Research

Dr. Huang's research interests are security, sensing as well as their intersection in the context of Internet-of-Things (IoT), which increasingly bonds Physical World, Cyberspace as well as Human Society together.
He leads a research group, namely PHY Group, aiming at exploring novel model-driven and data-driven technologies for Pervasive sensing, smart Healthcare, and perimeterless securitY. Thus, the main goals of PHY Group lie in three different yet interconnected domains:

  • Pervasive sensing for intelligent machines that demand high-level environmental understanding
  • Smart healthcare for aging people who need unobtrusive day-to-day health monitoring at home
  • Perimeterless security for distributed Cyberspaces where predefined encryption trust fails

Currently, PHY group focuses on the following directions:

  • RIS-Aided Physical Layer Security
  • Cross-Modal User Authentication
  • IoT Device Fingerprinting
  • Wi-Fi/mmWave-Based Human Behavior Sensing

主要研究方向：普适感知、智慧医疗和零信任安全。
当前研究课题：

  • 智能反射面辅助的物理层安全
  • 跨模态用户认证
  • IoT设备指纹识别
  • 基于Wi-Fi/毫米波的人体行为感知
